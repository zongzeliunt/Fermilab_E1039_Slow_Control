How to Change Warning Threshold

The warning threshold is defined in lines 67-186 of index_sub.js. Please modify these lines when you need to change the thresholds.

For example, here is the definitions for V & I of D1U; 

SetupOneGroup5(npt, data[ 0], "d1u" , "V", 1e-3, "kV"   , 1.25, -0.10, -0.05, +0.15, +0.20);
SetupOneGroup4(npt, data[ 1], "d1u" , "I", 0.01, "μA", 0.00,  0.00,  1.00,  2.00);
In the "SetupOneGroup5" function for the high voltage, the last 5 numbers "1.25, -0.10, -0.05, +0.15, +0.20" mean "V0, dV_l2, dV_l1, dV_h1, dV_h2", where
  • V0 + dV_l2 ... Lower threshold for error (red)
  • V0 + dV_l1 ... Lower threshold for warning (yellow)
  • V0 + dV_h1 ... Higher threshold for warning (yellow)
  • V0 + dV_h2 ... Higher threshold for error (red)
In the "SetupOneGroup4" function for the leak current, the last 4 numbers "0.00, 0.00, 1.00, 2.00" mean "I_l2, I_l1, I_h1, I_h2", where
  • I_l2 ... Lower threshold for error (red)
  • I_l1 ... Lower threshold for warning (yellow)
  • I_h1 ... Higher threshold for warning (yellow)
  • I_h2 ... Higher threshold for error (red)
